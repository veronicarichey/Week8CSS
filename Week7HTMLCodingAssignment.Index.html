You're from Where?

When people think of New Mexicans they may picture hikers, skiers, and hot air balloon enthusiast but never scuba divers. But there are those rare New Mexicans who love the land, the skies, and the water. So if you love scuba diving and live in New Mexico, you came to the right place. Scuba Divers of New Mexico is club of people of all ages, sizes, and skill level that love to scuba dive and are looking to meet other individuals who are passionate about scuba diving. We share scuba diving tips, arrange meetups at local diving spots, such as Blue Hole, and plan trips to places all over the world. We call ourselves the Sunfish, named after the native green sunfish which can be found in New Mexico.
